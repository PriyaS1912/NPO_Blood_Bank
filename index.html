Welcome to the Indian Red Cross

Our Mission

The Indian Red Cross is committed to serving humanity by promoting health, safety, and dignity for all. We work to alleviate human suffering during emergencies, disasters, and crises, ensuring swift and effective humanitarian aid. Through a spirit of voluntary service, we strive to empower communities, advance healthcare, foster social inclusion, and uphold the core principles of impartiality, neutrality, and unity.

National Blood Inventory

The Indian Red Cross plays a critical role in maintaining a national blood inventory, ensuring a steady and reliable supply of blood for those in need. Our nationwide network of blood banks and donation drives ensures that hospitals and emergency services have access to life-saving blood products. We work closely with volunteers and donors to make sure that blood is available in times of emergency, supporting both routine and critical medical needs.
