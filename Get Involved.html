Membership

Individuals and institutions can enroll in different grades of membership by subscribing to the following membership fees:

Membership Type Fee
Patron Rs. 25,000.00
Vice-Patron Rs. 12,000.00
Life Member Rs. 1,000.00
Life Associate Rs. 250.00
Annual Member Rs. 100.00 per annum
Annual Associate Rs. 50.00 per annum
Institutional Member Rs. 5,000.00 per annum

All members are associated with the Red Cross worldwide. Membership certificates are issued by the national headquarters, signed by the Honorable President of India for Patron memberships and by the Chairman and Secretary General for other memberships.

Members can attend the Annual General Meeting, presided over by the Honorable President of India, and contribute to the noble cause of serving the most vulnerable through the Red Cross.

Volunteer

Volunteering has been at the heart of the Red Cross since its inception in 1920. Volunteers are the backbone of all Indian Red Cross activities, helping branches to run successful programs and assisting millions in need.

The IRCS recruits volunteers irrespective of race, ethnicity, sex, religious belief, age, and disability. All volunteers are entitled to become a Member of the Indian Red Cross Society, receive appropriate training for their roles, and can accept or refuse any task in accordance with the code of ethics.

If you want to become a volunteer, please contact your nearest State/UT/District Red Cross branch.

Youth and Junior Red Cross

A substantial portion of the Indian Red Cross Society's membership consists of young people. Young volunteers can significantly contribute to meeting the needs of vulnerable individuals in their communities through the Red Cross youth program.

Programs can begin at the start of the school/college term, but local branches can provide more information at any time. Contact your district Red Cross branch or state Red Cross branch for guidance.
